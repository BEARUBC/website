Meet the UBC Bionics Team!

Our design team is subdivided into captain, leads, and general members -- all of whom are further categorized into their respective sub-teams.

Our Team

Faheem Saeed
Founder and Co-Captain

Faheem is a 4th-year mechanical engineering student at UBC. He is passionate about bionics and medical innovation to replace human physiology with bionics. He started the team to allow those at UBC passionate about bionics to further their passion.
Faheem is an avid modeler, renderer, and foodie.

Braeden Jury
Co-Captain

As Co-Captain, Braeden helps manage the 5 sub-teams to ensure consistent communication and progress, while providing design input and feedback. Braeden is a 5th-year engineering physics student and has been on the team since its inception, bringing experience from both the UBC Centre for Brain Health and Microsoft.
